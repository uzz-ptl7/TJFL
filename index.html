About Us

True Green Justice Foundation of Liberia (TJFL) is a registered non-profit dedicated to promoting environmental protection, sustainable agriculture, and social equity. Located in Buchanan City, Grand Bassa County, TJFL operates under the Associations Law of Liberia and is committed to empowering communities through education, advocacy, and community-led initiatives.
